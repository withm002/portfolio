About Me

Personal details

I'm a 24 year old Theology graduate originally from Horsham, West Sussex, now living in Peterborough, Cambridgeshire. I recently graduated from St. Mellitus College with a BA Hons in Theology, Ministry and Mission after serving as a placement student in a church in Peterborough for three years. I now work as a Junior Designer for one of the largest churches in the UK.

Ever since completing an A-Level in Graphic Design, with a focus on packaging and product design, I've been interested in and inspired by many aspects of design. Over the last couple of years i've had the opportunity of working on some great projects, both inside and outside of my daily job. Amongst other things, I hope to convey a creative but professional approach to producing high quality products with a style that is both modern and practical, as well as unique.

In my free time I enjoy exploring the Cambridgeshire wilderness with my wonderful girlfriend Amy. I also take time to contribute towards an ongoing collection of graphics for my Instagram at NVR ALN.
